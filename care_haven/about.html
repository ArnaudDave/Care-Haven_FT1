About Care Haven

Committed to enabling individuals with disabilities to reach their greatest potential

Our Story

We at Care Haven are committed to enabling individuals with disabilities to reach their greatest potential and live happy, fulfilled lives. We accomplish this through individualised training plans that are painstakingly created to foster critical life skills and independence. Our main goal is to establish a supportive and vibrant atmosphere where each person can flourish.

We think that genuine community involvement and inclusion have great power. Beyond only helping participants enhance their skills, our programs actively build relationships that allow them to feel like essential members of society. The cornerstones of our strategy are safety and support, which guarantee that each person at Care Haven feels safe, appreciated, and respected as they set out on their path to personal development.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with developmental and intellectual disabilities by providing a safe, engaging, and person-centered environment that fosters independence, personal growth, and community inclusion through meaningful day training programs.

Our Vision

To create a supportive and inclusive community where every individual is respected, valued, and celebrated for who they are.
