About Us

We at Care Haven are committed to enabling individuals with disabilities to reach their greatest potential and live happy, fulfilled lives. We accomplish this through individualized training plans designed to foster critical life skills and independence...

Mission

To empower individuals with developmental and intellectual disabilities by providing a safe, engaging, and person-centered environment...

Vision

To create a supportive and inclusive community where every individual is respected, valued, and celebrated for who they are...

Core Values

  • Dignity: Every person is worthy of meaningful participation in life.
  • Empowerment: We support growth, choice, and self-advocacy.
  • Safety: Emotional and physical well-being comes first, always.
  • Community: We foster belonging and connection in all we do.

Services

We provide Adult Day Training (ADT) services that support individuals with developmental disabilities in building daily living skills, enhancing communication, and promoting independence through person-centered programming...
